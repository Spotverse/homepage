How Spotverse Works
1
Select the Location

Users express interest by tapping on places on the map or selecting based on categories (All, Non-Alcoholic, Alcoholic, LGBTQ+, Live Music, etc.)

2
Explore the Place

Users can like or dislike profiles, applying various filters for search customization, including a particular area, drink type, age, gender, school, etc.

3
Match, Chat, and Meet

When two users express mutual interest in the same location and swipe right on each other profiles, a match occurs. They can start chatting and plan a meeting at that location.
